Meja Belajar Web Application

Meja Belajar is a web application designed to facilitate university students in becoming mentors or mentees. Mentors are students with excellent academic performance who assist other students in enhancing their academic excellence and mentees are students who are taught by mentors.

This application will offer features to connect mentors with mentees, fostering a supportive learning environment within the university community. I contributed as a Database engineer and Backend Developer in this project.
